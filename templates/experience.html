Detailed Experience

Stewardship Liaison

(June 2023-present)

First Nations of the Maa-nulth Treaty Society

  • Work in support of the five Maa-nulth modern treaty Nations: Huu-ay-aht First Nations, Ka:'yu:'k't'h'/Che:k'tles7et'h' First Nations, t̓uk̓ʷaa?atḥ Nation, Uchucklesaht Tribe and Yuułu?ił?atḥ Government.
  • Coordinate the development of a collective Maa-nulth Stewardship Network to monitor environmental, cultural and social values including traditional use species, habitats, biodiversity, archaeological sites and abiotic indicators across the traditional territory and Treaty lands
  • Develop briefings and recommendations to Maa-nulth First Nation leadership and Maa-nulth Treaty Society Board of Directors related to lands and resources work
  • Secure funding through federal, provincial and non-profit sources to support Maa-nulth Nations to pursue and implement self-determined and self-governed stewardship projects
  • Support the analysis of and response to federal and provincial engagement initiatives related to land and water stewardship. For example, BC's Wildlife Act Review, Biodiversity and Ecosystem Health Framework, Watershed Security Strategy and Disaster and Climate Risk and Resilience Assessment, and Canada's National Biodiversity Strategy, Framework for Aquatic Species at Risk Conservation, First Nations Climate Leadership Agenda and Right to a Healthy Environment Implementation.
  • Primary contact for major project impact assessments and liaise with federal departments to gather data and communicate information to the Maa-nulth Nations
  • Draft technical and strategic reports and responses on lands and resources issues for Nation leadership and government partners.

Wildlife Biostatistician

(April 2018-June 2023)

Fish & Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Forests

  • Provided expert advice on wildlife and habitat stewardship and management activities under the BC Wildlife Act
  • Supported stewardship decisions through data analyses, including population, harvest and mortality estimates
  • Interpreted and applied the Wildlife Act, and related regulations, policies and procedures, and the Species at Risk Act (Canada) as it relates to wildlife in BC
  • Advised decision makers and executive on wildlife, fish and habitat stewardship and licensing issues through briefing notes, presentations, and meetings
  • Assessed and monitored effectiveness of wildlife regulations, including in protected areas, through complex analyses using R software, MS Access/SQL, and custom software
  • Utilized wildlife habitat data, wildlife ranges and habitat requirements to make recommendations on wildlife stewardship activities
  • Led the Allocation Support Team to coordinate provincial wildlife allocation, including collaboration with regional staff, supporting First Nations engagement and co-management, and advising on wildlife allocation policies
  • Project manager and data lead for the Data & Licensing Transformation Project to modernize fish & wildlife data and licensing systems: identify and assess data needs to support decisions, prioritize business process improvement projects, data management, stakeholder engagement, internal and external training
  • Led the Fish & Wildlife Branch Open Data Strategy: led Branch working group, published fish and wildlife datasets, developed custom reports and communicated to both scientific and non-scientific audiences
  • Responsible for administering the guide outfitter program: harvest analysis and quota recommendations to statutory decision maker, guiding certificate tenures, licensing, liaising with clients
  • Developed and provided recommendations on policies, procedures, regulations, project plans, briefing notes and reports
  • Supported manager in meetings with First Nations on stewardship and data issues
  • Organized and participated in cross-Ministry and stakeholder meetings, including the Provincial Hunting and Trapping Advisory Team (PHTAT) and Guide Outfitters Association of BC (GOABC)
  • Angling data lead: Oracle and MS Access database management and improvement, angling data analysis and reporting
  • Participated in the review of harvest management and science for northern caribou, including data support, policy analysis and recommendations
  • Supervisor to the Wildlife Data Coordinator: responsible for establishing work priorities, MyPerformance, time and leave approvals, and mentoring
